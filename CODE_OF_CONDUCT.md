# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository owner.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions.
